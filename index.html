Once upon a time in a far far away place, there was a prince with eternal eyes and curly hair. Whom before wandering around this world, he enjoyed playing and perambulating the paradise and all the unimaginable.

He wanted to practice his favorite sport. He traveled then to the eternal snows and there with the polar bear, he started the fun. He called the penguins and won the match, but after the game his lonely starts.

The most beautiful love he wanted to know. So he traveled to this world and in the arms of his mother he founded this love. Now he is the ray of light of everyone he knows.
